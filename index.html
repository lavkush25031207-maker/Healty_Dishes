Our Story

We are a Pan-Asian chain that specialise in serving authentic, flavourful dishes from different parts of Asia. Our journey began in 2019 when two friends with a passion for food and a vision for the culinary industry came together. With no prior experience, we started small but had a big dream to create an experience that would take our customers on a journey through the streets of Asia.

Today, we are proud to say that we have grown from a single small kitchen to five outlets across India. Operating currently with five outlets and an additional five in the opening phase, our commitment to quality and authentic flavours has garnered a loyal customer base. Excitingly, our vision extends even further into the future, with plans to open 10 more outlets in 2024. We are thrilled to continue expanding and bringing our love for Asian cuisine to new locations.
